About KidMix:

KidMix is a FREE social network for children to communicate, learn, and share thoughts & photos with their friends and family. The KidMix platform and playdate scheduler was designed for children by parents. KidMix takes security seriously and requires users to have their identity verified. We ensure all kids are communicating in a private network.   KidMix is available on iOS, Android, and Web Browser providing cross platform communication for parents and kids.
