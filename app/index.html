Why KidMix?

Safely communicate with Friends Online

Features

Safe & Secure Kid’s Network with Parental Monitoring

KidMix is a private social network designed for kids. Kidmix takes the necessary steps to meet the Child Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) standards and requires Parental ID verification for all users. The KidMix Parent dashboard provides full oversight of all your child’s activities. The dashboard is available on a web browser or via the mobile KidMix Parent App.

Playdate Scheduler

Kids simply schedule their own playdates with Parental Confirmation. Kidmix's Playdate planner takes the burden off parents to coordinate the logistics of their child's playdates.

Communicate with Friends without bullying

Kids can post status updates, share photos, and send fun emojis to their friends! Their friends can comment and like their messages all in a safe and anonymous manner.

Photo Sharing

Kids can post their own photos once approved by their parents. They can express themselves and get to know classmates and friends which can help form lifelong bonds!

Calendar

Online Calendar for your kids to keep track of their playdates and other planned events. Parents can update their kid’s calendar to track parties, practices, and other important events.

SmartZone

A collection of curated educational & fun videos for kids. All videos are pre-approved to ensure they are kid friendly. Kids can save their favorites and share with their friends.

Group Creator

Group your child’s friends, teammates, and other buddies for easy and direct communication between kids. Ideal for classmates, sports teams, neighborhood friends, clubs, and other groups.
