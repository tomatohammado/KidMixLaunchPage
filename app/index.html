Features

Safe & Secure Kid’s Network with Parental Monitoring

All Parents are ID verified before they can create online profiles for their children. The parent dashboard provides full oversight of all your child’s online activity.

Playdate Scheduler

Kids simply schedule their own playdates with Parental Confirmation.

Communicate with Friends without bullying

Kids can post their own status updates and like or comment on their friend’s posts in an anonymous way. Our platform helps ensure a no negativity/non-bullying environment by using our built in commenting system.

Photo Sharing/Editing & Custom Characters

Kids can post their own photos once approved by their parents. Our optional Photo editor and Character Creator allows them to add fun stickers and create their own custom characters to share!

Calendar

Online Calendar for your kids to keep track of their playdates and other planned events. Parents can update their kid’s calendar to track parties, practices, and other important events.

SmartZone

A collection of educational/entertaining kid safe videos that kids can access. Kids can save and share their favorites with their friends.

Group Creator

Group your child’s friends, teammates, classmates, clubs, and other buddies to enable easy and direct communication.
